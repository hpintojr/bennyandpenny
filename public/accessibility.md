# Accessibility Statement

Canonical page: https://www.bennyandpenny.com/accessibility

Benny & Penny's is working to make this website usable by the widest possible audience, including people who use assistive technologies.

## Accessibility target

We use WCAG 2.2 Level AA as our design and engineering target. Accessibility is an ongoing process of testing, improving, and remediating barriers as they are identified.

## Accessibility preferences

Use the Accessibility preferences button in the site footer to adjust text size, high contrast, motion, and font readability for this device. These optional controls work alongside browser and operating-system accessibility settings.

## Current work

- Keyboard-accessible navigation, forms, links, and interactive controls
- Visible focus indicators, readable contrast, responsive reflow, and reduced-motion support
- Meaningful headings, landmarks, labels, alternative text, and status messages
- Automated checks plus manual keyboard, screen-reader, contrast, and zoom testing
- Ongoing review of new site content and features

## Known limitations

This portfolio is actively being reviewed against WCAG 2.2 Level AA. Some third-party websites linked from this portfolio, embedded services, and externally hosted content may not be fully controlled by Benny & Penny's. Barriers within our control will be reviewed and addressed when reported or discovered.

## Request help or report a barrier

Email: hello@bennyandpenny.com

Include the page or feature involved, the assistive technology or browser you are using when relevant, and the support you need. We aim to acknowledge accessibility requests within five business days and will work with you to identify a reasonable alternative format or assistance where needed.
