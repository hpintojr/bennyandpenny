# About Hamilton Pinto Jr.

> The big picture, executed at the technical level.

Canonical page: https://bennyandpenny.com/about

Benny & Penny's is named for Hamilton Pinto Jr.'s children and serves as the banner for his work as a software architect, creative technologist, business consultant, and publisher.

## A disciplined problem solver with a designer's eye

Hamilton's traditional training began at Westwood College of Technology, where he studied Graphic Design and Multimedia, and he holds a BA in Visual Communications. This creative foundation supports his work in custom technology, design, and publishing.

Hamilton architects and builds custom solutions, integrates third-party and proprietary APIs, and implements systems that improve operational efficiency, data flow, and business intelligence while aligning creative execution with business objectives.

As owner of Pinto Design Group and Pinto Consulting, he has opened and supported more than 60 establishments across financial services, restaurants, law, medicine, and professional services throughout Southern California. His studies also include certifications from Point Loma Nazarene University and advanced work in blockchain and cryptocurrency applications through UC Berkeley's Haas School of Business.
